# Get Your Project Online 🌍

This little kit lets you put your project on the **real internet** — a link you can text to a friend, share on social media, or show your family — just by **asking Claude**. You don't need to know what Git is. You don't need to learn any commands. You just talk, and Claude does the rest.

## One-time setup (do this once)

1. **Add the kit to your project.** Open your project folder, then paste this **one line** into the terminal and press enter:

   ```
   curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lior25659567/vibe-deploy/main/install.sh | bash
   ```

   This is the *only* command you'll ever type. It drops the kit into your project for you.

2. **Quit Claude Code and open it again.** This lets it notice the new files. (Just close it fully and reopen — that's it.)

3. **Type `/mcp`** and press enter. A browser window will pop open asking you to **sign in to Netlify** (Netlify is the service that hosts your site — it's free). Sign in. If it also asks you to **sign in to GitHub**, do that too.

That's the whole setup. You only ever do this once. 🎉

## Everyday use (forever after)

Whenever you want to put your project online or update it, just tell Claude in plain words:

> **"Put my project online."**

or, after you've made changes:

> **"Update my live site."**

That's it. Claude handles all the behind-the-scenes work and hands you back a link. No commands, no dashboards, no clicking around.

## If something looks wrong

Don't worry — you can't break anything. If a link doesn't work, the page looks empty, or you see a message you don't understand, **just tell Claude what you see** ("my site is blank" or "I got an error that says…") and it'll figure out the fix for you. Talking to Claude is always the answer.
